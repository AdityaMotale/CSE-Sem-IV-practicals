Marathwada Institute of Technology

"Marathwada Institute of Technology has been established in 1984 to cater the technical education in the rural part of Maharashtra. The institution spreads over 13 acres of land with separate hostel facilities for girls and boys. This Institute is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and is affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere, Raigarh, Maharashatra, the state technical university popularly known as BATU. The institution offers B. Tech. programmes in five disciplines and an Architecture program. Post-Graduation programs are also offered in Engineering & Technology, Computer Applications and Architecture. The Institute has a University recognized Ph.D. Research Center in Mechanical Engineering and Civil Engineering. More than 2500 full time & part time students are on campus for availing graduate and post graduate education and doing Ph.D. research. The institute has excellent quality infrastructure with well-equipped laboratories. Well qualified faculties from IITs, NITs and reputed organizations are available in the institute. The faculty members are motivated and dedicated to create and provide a conducive learning environment in the Institute. Institute participates in national and international exhibitions and presents students projects and research contributions/activities where the institute has been recognized by the organizers and won prizes at national level. Students are encouraged to actively participate in National and State level co-curricular and extracurricular activities like SAE BAJA, SUPRA, ROBOCON etc."   

Department of Computer Science and Engineering

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering is committed to providing quality education to the students through innovative methods of teaching and practical orientation. We are working hard to develop our students as leaders, innovators and technologists at international level. The department has highly qualified and experienced faculty and we are continuously working on improving the quality of teaching and learning to impart high standard education to the students in comprehensible ways. The department has excellent infrastructure which provides an encouraging environment for achieving academic excellence.The department provides industry driven trainings and international certifications through its strong industrial collaborations. We regularly conduct workshops, trainings and technical talks for faculties and students to keep them acquainted with latest technological developments. We focus on improving the soft skills, aptitude and overall personality development of the students to make them globally competitive. The department provides healthy environment for interaction amongst its stakeholders.
